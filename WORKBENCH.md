## How to Claim

1. **Check this file first** - Is someone already working on it?
2. **Add your row** to Active Claims
3. **Commit:** `git commit -m "claim: [what you're working on]"`
4. **Push** to your branch

## How to Release

1. Create your PR
2. Move your row from Active → Recently Completed
3. Commit and push
